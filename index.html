OUR STORY

Seattle Coffee Company was created by a husband and wife team – Ally and Scott Svenson – whose inspiration was born out of their experience of specialty coffee brands in their hometown of Seattle. They had been living in London for years, missing the Seattle way of life, and frustrated that a concept they took for granted back home was not available in the UK.

Seattle Coffee Company opens in London, sparking a coffee revolution in the UK.

Seattle Coffee Company opens in Cavendish Square, Cape Town, SA

Starbucks buys out Seattle UK, converting all their stores to the familiar green Siren branding.

Seattle continues to thrive as a family-owned South African company, with over 85 stores countrywide.
